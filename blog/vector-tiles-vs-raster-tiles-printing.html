Vector Tiles vs Raster Tiles: What’s Better for Printing?

Reading time: ~6 min

Raster tiles

  • Pros: Simple to consume; predictable appearance.
  • Cons: Fixed resolution; can look soft at large print sizes.

Vector tiles

  • Pros: Scalable, styleable in the client; crisp lines.
  • Cons: More client work; styling must be considered for print.

Practical guidance

  • For sharp prints, keep your annotations as vectors.
  • When using raster basemaps, avoid extreme upscales—frame the print at native zoom levels.
  • Use Show Print Area in TrueGIS to preview coverage before export.
