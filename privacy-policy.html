Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At TrueGIS, your privacy matters. TrueGIS Maps does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The platform runs entirely in your browser — no accounts, no cloud syncing, and no personal tracking.

Cookies & Tracking

TrueGIS Maps does not use cookies or local storage to track users. We use lightweight analytics to understand general traffic trends. These are anonymous and do not collect any identifiable data.

Third-Party Services

Ads may be served through Google AdSense. While TrueGIS does not store or access personal data, Google may use cookies to display relevant ads. You can read more in Google's Advertising Policies .

Your Rights

Because TrueGIS Maps does not store personal data, there's nothing to access or erase. If you have any questions about privacy, feel free to reach out at rikesh.lad@truegis.co.uk.

Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated over time. Any changes will be posted here with the revised “Last updated” date.
