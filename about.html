About TrueGIS Maps

TrueGIS Maps is a modern, browser-based mapping tool designed for fast, intuitive use directly in your browser. No installs, no accounts — just clean, responsive tools for creating and printing maps.

Whether you're marking areas, sketching routes, or preparing field-ready printouts, TrueGIS Maps gives you full control over style and layout.

  • Draw and customize points, lines, and polygons with flexible color, opacity, and width settings
  • Print exact-scale PDFs using Inkmap with built-in scale and layout options
  • Search locations, use your current position, and quickly zoom to places
  • Export your map in high-quality formats including PNG and standard geodata formats

Created by TrueGIS, the platform focuses on privacy, performance, and ease of use — perfect for educators, planners, field workers, and map enthusiasts.
